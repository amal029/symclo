get-sum-operands
(get-sum-operands x)
Gets all the operands of a nested sum expression. 

E.g.:  (def t (+ (+ 3 (* x y)) (* z 3))) 
(get-sum-operands (rest t)) = [3 (* x y) (* z 3)].
